What is Urban Gardening?

Urban gardening is growing plants in city environments using small spaces like balconies, windowsills, or rooftops. It's a great way to grow fresh food and connect with nature in urban areas.

Why Urban Garden?

Fresh Food

Grow your own herbs, vegetables, and fruits right at home for better nutrition and taste.

Environmental Benefits

Reduce carbon footprint and improve air quality while creating habitat for beneficial insects.

Getting Started

Choose a sunny spot with 4-6 hours of sunlight daily. Start with easy plants like herbs, lettuce, or cherry tomatoes in containers with good drainage.

You'll need basic supplies: pots, potting soil, seeds, and a watering can. Begin small and expand as you learn.

Quick Tips

  • Start with easy-to-grow herbs like basil and mint
  • Water regularly but don't overwater
  • Use containers with drainage holes
